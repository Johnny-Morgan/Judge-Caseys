Judge Casey’s Pizza - with Ham & Arugula

Ingredients: Pizza dough or pre-packaged 10' Pizza base - or bigger if you have a big family or lots of friends, adjust amounts to compensate. Two tablespoons thinly slivered red onion. A teaspoon sliced Fresno chilli (or jalapenos) A generous layer of ham. A ½ cup Parmesan cheese A tablespoon and a half Judge Casey’s ½ a cup Arugula ½ a Zucchini or summer squash - olive oil, lemon, salt and pepper.
